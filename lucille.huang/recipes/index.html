Three Recipes that I often make

Nabeki Udon

Ingredients

  • Udon 200g
  • Onion 5g
  • Slice beef x6
  • Egg x1

    • Steps

  • 1. Boiling the water
  • 2. Put Udon and cook 2 minutes
  • 3. Put beef and cook 1 minute
  • 4. Boil the Egg and cook 4 minutes
  • 5. Put Onions on the top
